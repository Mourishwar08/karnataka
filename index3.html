Wonderla - Thrilling Fun Awaits!

Wonderla Amusement Park is one of the largest amusement parks in India. With thrilling rides, water slides, and wave pools, it's the perfect destination for family and friends.

Famous Foods of Karnataka

Masala Dosa

A crispy rice pancake filled with spiced potato, served with chutney and sambhar.

Ragi Mudde

Finger millet balls served with spicy curry, a nutritious Karnataka staple.

Mysore Pak

A sweet treat made of ghee, sugar, and gram flour, originating from Mysore.

Traditional Dances of Karnataka

Yakshagana

A vibrant folk theatre combining dance, music, and dialogue.

Dollu Kunitha

A drum-based dance performed by men in a group.

Heritage Sites of Karnataka

Hampi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its ancient temples and ruins.

Badami Caves

Rock-cut temples with intricate carvings from the 6th century.
